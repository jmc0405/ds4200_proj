Regression: Listening Time vs Spending

Analysis

The regression plot examines the relationship between hours of music listening and average concert ticket spending. Although there is a slight upward trend indicating that students who listen to more music may spend slightly more on concerts, the relationship is relatively weak. The wide spread of data points shows that students with similar listening habits often spend very different amounts, suggesting that factors beyond listening time such as artist preference, social influence, or personal budget play a more significant role in determining concert spending.
