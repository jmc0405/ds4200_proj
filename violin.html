Distribution of Daily Music Listening Hours by School

Analysis

This violin plot compares the distribution of daily music listening hours across schools. It shows not only which schools tend to have higher or lower listening time on average, but also how spread out the responses are within each group. The embedded box plots highlight the median and quartiles, while the individual points show the exact student responses.
