Listening Hours by School

Analysis

The violin plot compares the distribution of daily music listening hours across different schools. It highlights not only the average listening behavior within each school, but also the spread and concentration of responses. Some schools show a tighter cluster of listening hours, while others have a wider distribution, suggesting more variation in music engagement. Overall, the visualization helps show how listening behavior differs across academic groups while still revealing that most students fall within a similar general range of daily listening time.
