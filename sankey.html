Favorite Genre to Concert Attendance Genre

Analysis

The Sankey diagram shows how students’ favorite music genres translate into the genres of concerts they attend. While there is a clear tendency for students to attend concerts that match their preferred genre especially for popular categories like Pop, EDM, and Hip-hop—there is also significant cross-genre movement. In particular, Pop acts as a central hub, with listeners attending a wide range of different concert types. This suggests that although students have defined music preferences, their live music behavior is more flexible and exploratory.
